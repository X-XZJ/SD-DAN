# ALDD9
The apple leaf disease dataset adopted in this study consists of two publicly available datasets and one self-collected dataset, which are specified as follows:
AppleLeaf9 Dataset : This dataset covers eight common apple leaf diseases, including Alternaria leaf spot, brown spot, frogeye leaf spot, grey spot, mosaic disease, powdery mildew, rust and scab, with a total of 14,582 images, approximately 94% of which were captured under real field conditions. In this work, 4,427 images were selected from the dataset and reannotated following a unified annotation specification.
ATLDSD Dataset : Four types of apple leaf diseases are contained in this dataset: Alternaria leaf spot, grey spot, brown spot and rust. To address the shortage of grey spot samples in the AppleLeaf9 dataset, an additional 206 grey spot images were selected and reannotated.
Since existing public datasets lack samples of apple anthracnose, our research group collected 345 leaf images of apple anthracnose in complex natural field environments at the Taihang Mountain Experimental Base of Hebei Agricultural University to serve as supplementary data.
By integrating all the above data, we constructed the ALDD9 dataset (Apple Leaf Disease Dataset 9), which contains a total of 4,978 images covering nine prevalent apple leaf diseases. All images were annotated with the LabelImg tool under the guidance of agricultural experts and then converted into the COCO data format uniformly.
